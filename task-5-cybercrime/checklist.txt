Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
---------------------------------------------
| |Do's | |Don'ts |
---------------------------------------------
| |* Do keep your profile professional by using a clear, high-quality headshot. | |* Don't post confidential information about your university, projects, or internships. |
---------------------------------------------
| |* Do use privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts and photos. | |* Don't engage in "doom-scrolling" or online arguments that could damage your digital reputation. |
---------------------------------------------
| |* Do share your academic achievements, certifications, and technical projects. | |* Don't use offensive language or share unverified "fake news" and misinformation. |
---------------------------------------------
| |* Do network meaningfully by personalizing connection requests to industry peers. | |* Don't tag others in photos or posts without their explicit permission. |
---------------------------------------------
| |* Do double-check your grammar and tone before posting to ensure clarity. | |* Don't post vent-style complaints about professors, classmates, or employers. |
