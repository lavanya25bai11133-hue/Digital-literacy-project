While researching cybercrime prevention, what surprised me most was how common and simple many online scams are, especially UPI and phishing frauds. I learned that most cybercrimes do not happen due to advanced hacking, but because people unknowingly share OTPs, click fake links, or trust messages that look official. Even educated students can fall for such scams if they are careless or in a hurry.
One habit I will personally change is being more alert while making online payments and checking links before clicking on them. I will also avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or UPI transactions. This research made me realize that staying safe online does not require technical expertise, but awareness, patience, and responsible digital behavior.
